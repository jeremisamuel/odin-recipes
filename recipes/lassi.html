Mango Lassi

The Mango Lassi is a refreshing, creamy, and flavorful drink that perfectly blends the tropical sweetness of ripe mangoes with the richness of yogurt. The smooth, velvety texture is enhanced by a touch of cardamom, while a drizzle of honey or sugar balances the flavors. Served chilled, this classic Indian beverage offers a perfect harmony of fruity freshness, creamy indulgence, and aromatic warmth. Itâ€™s the kind of drink that cools you down, delights your taste buds, and keeps you coming back for more!

Ingredients!

  • Ripe Mangoes
  • Yogurt
  • Milk
  • Sugar or Honey
  • Cardamom Powder

Steps to Prepare!

  1. Peel and Chop
  2. Blend Ingredients
  3. Blend Until smooth
  4. Adjust Consistency
  5. Chill and Serve
