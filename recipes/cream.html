Crème Brûlée

Crème Brûlée is a rich, creamy, and indulgent dessert that perfectly balances silky smooth custard with the irresistible crunch of caramelized sugar. The velvety vanilla-infused custard melts in your mouth, while the crisp, golden caramel topping shatters with every spoonful, creating a symphony of textures. Whether enjoyed on its own or infused with hints of citrus, espresso, or liqueur, every bite is a perfect harmony of sweetness, creaminess, and warmth. It’s the kind of dessert that comforts the soul, delights the senses, and keeps you craving just one more spoonful!

Ingredients!

  • Heavy Cream
  • Egg Yolks
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Vanilla Bean Extract
  • Salt

Steps to Prepare!

  1. Heat the Cream
  2. Whisk Egg Yolks with Sugar
  3. Temper the Eggs
  4. Bake in a Water Bath
  5. Caramelize the Sugar
